Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about SparkConnect? We have answers to help you navigate our platform and hire trusted professionals.

General Questions

What is SparkConnect?
+
SparkConnect is Nigeria's premier connection platform matching homes and commercial businesses with top-tier, vetted, and verified electricians. We enable safe, fast, and direct communication without intermediary service fees.
How does SparkConnect work?
+
You can browse our directory of electricians, filter by state/location or specialty, read reviews from previous customers, and inspect their portfolios. Once you find a suitable match, you can connect with them directly via Phone Call or WhatsApp to discuss and negotiate your project.
Is SparkConnect free to use?
+
Yes, SparkConnect is 100% free for homeowners and businesses looking for electricians. We do not charge booking fees or take a commission from the electricians' earnings.

For Customers

Are the electricians verified?
+
Yes, all professionals listed on SparkConnect undergo verification processes including credential checks, identification verification, and references. We encourage users to check ratings and read detailed reviews before hiring.
What should I do in case of an electrical emergency?
+
You can filter electricians on our search page for those specializing in "Emergency Callouts" or "Residential Wiring" who are closest to your location. Always turn off your main power switch immediately if there is active smoke, fire, or sparks.
How can I leave a review?
+
Simply navigate to the electrician's public profile page, click on the "Write a Review" section, choose a rating star, enter your name, and write your honest feedback. Your review will help build trust in our community.

For Electricians

How do I join SparkConnect as an electrician?
+
To get listed, click on the "Create Account" or "Join as an Electrician" button. Select your state, specialty role, description, and upload a profile picture. Once registered, you will have access to a dashboard where you can edit your profile, display your phone number, and manage your portfolio gallery.
How do I gain more visibility and clients?
+
To stand out, ensure your profile is fully complete. Add high-quality images of your completed projects to your gallery, request reviews from clients you've worked with, and maintain a highly professional profile description.
