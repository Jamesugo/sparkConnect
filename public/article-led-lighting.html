Lighting accounts for a surprising percentage of your monthly electricity bill. In Nigeria, swapping out old, inefficient bulbs is one of the fastest ways to save money and reduce the load on your inverter or generator.

The Reign of LED Bulbs

LED (Light Emitting Diode) bulbs are the undisputed champions of efficiency. They use up to 90% less energy than traditional incandescent bulbs and last up to 25 times longer. While they cost slightly more to buy, they pay for themselves in energy savings within months.

CFLs: The Middle Ground

CFL (Compact Fluorescent Light) bulbs were the original energy savers before LEDs took over. They are cheaper than LEDs but contain small amounts of mercury, meaning they require careful disposal if broken.
