Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact an electrician? +
You can browse electrician profiles, select a professional, and connect with them directly via Phone Call or WhatsApp message. No intermediate booking fees are required!
Are the electricians on SparkConnect verified? +
Yes! We verify our registered electricians by checking their past project galleries, customer ratings, and contact details. Look for the "Top Rated" or verified badges on their profiles.
How much does it cost to hire an electrician? +
Pricing varies depending on the complexity and size of the job. You can use our "Quick Cost Estimator" widget above to get a baseline market rate estimation for typical tasks in Nigeria.
Can I register as an electrician? +
Absolutely! Click "Create Account" or "Sign In" in the navigation menu, fill in your details, select your specialty and state, and build your profile portfolio to start receiving client calls.
