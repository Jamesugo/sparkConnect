Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hire an electrician?
+
Simply use the search bar or browse our services to find professionals in your area. You can view their profiles, compare ratings, and contact them directly via phone or WhatsApp to book.
How are electricians verified?
+
We perform thorough checks including identity verification, skill assessments based on past projects, and background checks before awarding a verified badge.
Can I book emergency services?
+
Yes! Many of our electricians offer 24/7 emergency callouts. You can filter by emergency services or look for professionals who highlight rapid response in their profiles.
How do payments work?
+
SparkConnect charges zero commission fees. You negotiate the price and pay the electrician directly for their services.
