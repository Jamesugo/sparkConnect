About SparkConnect

We are Nigeria's leading platform connecting households and commercial businesses with verified, reliable, and professional electricians.

Our Journey

Bridging the Gap in Electrical Solutions

Making Electrical Connections Safe and Simple

SparkConnect was founded with a single mission: to create a trusted network of verified electrical professionals in Nigeria. We recognized the challenges that homeowners and businesses face when searching for skilled electricians, as well as the difficulty honest electricians have in finding clients.

By offering a simple, transparent, and direct directory platform, we eliminate intermediate booking fees and allow clients to connect directly with professionals via Phone Call or WhatsApp.
