Safety

Safety Guidelines

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to stay protected.

Important Notice If you are experiencing an electrical emergency such as sparks, burning smells, or a power outage affecting critical equipment, please contact your local emergency services immediately before using SparkConnect.

For Customers: Before the Job

  • Always hire electricians with a verified SparkConnect badge — they have passed our identity and skills checks.
  • Review the electrician's profile, ratings, and past reviews before making contact.
  • Agree on the full scope of work and pricing in writing (via WhatsApp or message) before the job starts.
  • Do not share your bank account details, PINs, or passwords with any electrician.
  • Ensure someone else is home during the visit if possible, especially for first-time engagements.

For Customers: During the Job

  • Ensure the main power supply is switched off before any electrical work begins.
  • Do not attempt to assist or interfere with electrical work unless you are qualified.
  • Keep children and pets away from the work area.
  • Ask the electrician to explain what they are doing, especially for large jobs.
  • Request a receipt or written record of all completed work and parts used.

For Electricians: Professional Standards

  • Only accept jobs within your area of expertise — do not take on work beyond your skill level.
  • Always carry valid identification and present it to the customer at the start of every job.
  • Use correct PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) at all times.
  • Follow all applicable electrical codes, regulations, and safety standards in Nigeria.
  • Never perform electrical work in wet or unsafe conditions without appropriate precautions.
  • Test all work thoroughly before handover and inform the customer of any unresolved risks.

For Electricians: Conduct & Reviews

  • Treat all customers with respect and professionalism at all times.
  • Be punctual. If you cannot make an appointment, notify the customer as early as possible.
  • Do not solicit fake reviews — this will result in immediate removal from the platform.
  • Report any safety concerns or dangerous conditions you observe to the customer promptly.

Reporting Issues

If you encounter any of the following, please report it to SparkConnect immediately:

  • An electrician misrepresenting their qualifications or credentials
  • Any form of fraud, harassment, or unsafe behaviour
  • Suspicious account activity or fake reviews
