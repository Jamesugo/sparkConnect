How to Protect Your Appliances from Power Surges

By SparkConnect Editorial•5 min read•October 2026
Back to Home

Power surges are sudden spikes in electrical voltage that can fry the delicate circuits in your TV, refrigerator, and laptops in milliseconds. In a country where the grid is notoriously unstable, protection is non-negotiable.

Automatic Voltage Regulators (AVRs)

An AVR acts as a shield between the grid and your appliances. When the voltage drops too low or spikes too high, the AVR stabilizes it, ensuring only safe power reaches your devices.

Whole-House Surge Protectors

Instead of buying a stabilizer for every single appliance, a whole-house surge protector is installed directly at your electrical panel. This protects everything in the house simultaneously.
