Inverters have become a staple in Nigerian homes, offering a silent and eco-friendly alternative to generators. But with so many options, how do you choose the right one?

Understanding Inverter Capacities (kVA)

The capacity of an inverter is measured in kilovolt-amperes (kVA). A 1kVA inverter is usually enough for basic lighting, a TV, and fans. For refrigerators and air conditioners, you'll need 3kVA to 5kVA or more. Always calculate your total wattage before making a purchase.

Battery Types: Tubular vs Lithium-ion

Batteries are the heart of your inverter system. Tubular batteries are affordable and reliable, but they require maintenance and have a shorter lifespan. Lithium-ion batteries, while more expensive upfront, last much longer, charge faster, and require zero maintenance.
